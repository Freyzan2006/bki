Institute management

Director of the Institute of Bioorganic Chemistry of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan

Turaev Abbaskhan Sabirkhanovich

Abbaskhan Sabirkhanovich Turaev – Doctor of Chemical Sciences (1993), Professor (1994), Academician of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan (2018). Honored Inventor and Innovator of the Republic of Uzbekistan (2003), Order of the Fidokorona Khizmatlari Uchun (2012).

Graduated from Tashkent State University (1970).

Head of the laboratory of the Research Institute of Chemistry and Technology of Cotton Cellulose (1984 - 1993), head of the laboratory of the Institute of Bioorganic Chemistry of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan (1993 - 1996), Deputy Director for Scientific Work of the Institute of Bioorganic Chemistry of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan (1996 - 2006), Director of the Institute of Bioorganic Chemistry Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan (2012 - 2017), Deputy Director for Scientific Work of the Institute of Bioorganic Chemistry of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan (2017-2022).

From 2022 to the present, Director of the Institute of Bioorganic Chemistry.

Under the leadership of A.S. Turaev conducted comprehensive research on the targeted chemical modification of polysaccharides - cellulose, pectin, galactomannans, amylose and hydrophilic copolymers, acrylic series. On their basis, macromolecular systems with various functional groups and molecular parameters were obtained. As a result of these studies, more than twenty new original drugs have been developed and introduced into medical practice (Safinol, Sulfogel - anticoagulants, Savinal - blood substitute, Nitrocel - antianginal, Biomayrin - anti-tuberculosis, Polygel - ultrasound gel, Timogel, Bactocel - wound healing, Fludigel - against dermatitis) and surgical products: absorbable threads (Kacelon) and non-absorbable (Biolon Ra, Biolon Re, Biolon Rs), absorbable hemostatic materials (Hemocel, Hemosponge, Bioplant) and products in contact with blood (embolic polymer material, Geletcel and Chlorocel for treatment of purulent wounds). Five of these drugs are produced by pharmaceutical enterprises of the Republic of Uzbekistan on the basis of licensing agreements.

Author of more than 350 scientific papers, 21 copyright certificates, 35 patents.

Trained 4 doctors and 16 candidates of science.

Deputy Director for Science

Oshchepkova Yulia Igorevna

Yulia Igorevna Oshchepkova – Doctor of Chemical Sciences (2016), Professor (2023).

Medal “Shukhrat” (2020), Badge “Leader of Innovative Ideas” (2022).

Graduated from the National University of Uzbekistan (2005).

Head of the Laboratory of Chemistry of Proteins and Peptides (2017-2022).

From 2022 to the present, Deputy Director for Science of the Institute of Bioorganic Chemistry.

Yu.I. Oshchepkova is a specialist in bioorganic chemistry, in particular in the field of chemistry of low-molecular bioregulators, proteins and peptides (study of the structure and mechanism of function) of plants and animals.

Yu.I. Oshchepkova was the first to isolate and study a number of new antimicrobial peptides from seeds and seedlings of Nigella sativa. The obtained data on the lipid transfer protein Ns-LTP1 are registered in the EMBL p86527 database. Based on antimicrobial peptides from nigella seeds, the pharmaceutical composition “Sedanil” was created, which has wound-healing properties in the case of purulent-septic complications in thermal burns and mechanical wounds. A comprehensive technology for using Nigella sativa seeds in a low-waste manner has been developed, which makes it possible to increase the profitability of the work and reduce the cost of production. From seed residues, a technology has been developed for producing the lipolytic enzyme “Niglesim”, which causes hydrolytic breakdown (decomposition with the participation of water) of fats of plant and animal origin. This enzyme is planned to be produced as a dietary supplement in the form of enteric tablets and capsules.

Under her leadership, a technology for obtaining cardiolipin antigen substance was developed and a set of reagents Cardiolipin Antigen for the microprecipitation reaction “AgCl-RMP” was introduced into production, intended for use in clinical diagnostic laboratories for the diagnosis of syphilis.

Автор 240 публикаций (из них 50 за рубежом), 10 патентов, 2 товAuthor of 240 publications (50 of them abroad), 10 patents, 2 trademarks.арных знаков.

Prepared 3 PhDs.
